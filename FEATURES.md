# SkillSetu — Implemented Features Catalog 🚀

SkillSetu is a premium, AI-powered career mastery platform designed for engineering professionals. Below is a comprehensive list of the features implemented to date.

## 🧠 Core Learning Engine
- **Exponential Skill Decay**: Implemented a scientific forgetting curve (`mastery = mastery × e^(−λt)`) that automatically degrades your skill scores over time if you don't practice.
- **Weighted Mastery Scoring**: Accuracy, consistency (streaks), and question difficulty (1.0x to 1.5x) all influence your real-time mastery gain.
- **Career Fit Analyzers**: Real-time readiness calculation based on a weighted average of required skills for specific roles.
- **Priority-Driven Practice**: An engine that identifies which subskills need revision most based on their decay risk and mastery gap.

## 🤖 AI Mentor Layer (Gemini 1.5 Flash Integrated)
- **AI Career Navigator**: Suggests career paths based on user interests, strengths, and goals.
- **AI Learning Assistant**: Provides "Deep Dive" explanations for complex engineering concepts using analogies.
- **AI Subskill Mentors**: Three specialized personas directly on subskill pages:
    - **Doubt Solver**: Conceptual simplifiers with "real-world anchors".
    - **Resource Hub**: Advanced industry topic recommendations.
    - **Mastery Challenge**: Generates unique, extreme scenario-based questions on-demand.
- **AI Question Generation**: Automated procedural generation for thousands of rigorous, topic-specific practice questions.

## 📊 Performance & Personalization
- **Interactive Dashboards**: Heatmap visualizations for activity, mastery radar charts for career coverage, and weekly progress trackers.
- **Career Readiness Dashboard**: High-level overview of "Best Fit" careers and prioritized "Needs Revision" alerts.
- **Adaptive Difficulty**: Questions and resources served based on the user's current mastery level.
- **User Settings**: Persistent profile management, including weekly goal setting and target career selection.

## 🎮 Gamification & Engagement
- **Consistency Streaks**: Fire-animated trackers for daily practice goals.
- **Automated Badge Engine**: Milestone-based achievement system (Early Bird, Deep Diver, Mastery Milestones).
- **Celebration Effects**: High-performance UI triggers (confetti, achievement modals) for significant mastery gains.

## 📁 Portfolio & Opportunities (The Proof Vault)
- **Proof-of-Mastery**: Automated logging of high-performance scores and milestones as verifiable achievements.
- **Project Uploads**: Support for attaching simulation files, notebooks, and reports to your skill profile.
- **Internship Mapping**: Real-time eligibility tracking for internships based on specific skill mastery levels and gap analysis.
- **Shareable Portfolio**: Verifiable, public-facing link to showcase your engineering mastery to recruiters.

## 🏗️ Technical Architecture & Data
- **High-Scale Engineering Catalog**: Extensive data for **Process Engineer**, **Bioprocess Engineer**, **Chemical Safety Engineer**, **Bioinformatics Scientist**, **Software Engineer**, and **Data Analyst**.
- **Hydrated Practice Bank**: 800+ unique, scenario-based practice questions and 300+ hand-picked learning resources.
- **Responsive Navigation**: Premium mobile-first design with animated hamburger menus and breadcrumbs.
- **Theme-Aware UI**: Dynamic dark mode support for all interactive components, including D3-powered skill graphs.
- **Performance Optimized**: Server-side rendering (SSR) for SEO, lazy-loading for heavy visuals, and loading skeletons for perceived speed.
